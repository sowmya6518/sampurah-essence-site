Why Sampurah Essence?

Because your family deserves food that feels like home, not factory.

Sampurah Essence started with one simple thought: “If we won’t serve it to our own family, we won’t send it to yours.”

  • Small batch cooking.
    We don’t mass produce – every batch is prepared fresh, in limited quantity.
  • Ingredients you recognise.
    No premixes, no artificial colour, no taste enhancers – just kitchen staples you trust.
  • Recipes rooted in tradition.
    Inspired by Andhra & Godavari-style home cooking, passed down across generations.
Our Promise Clean, honest, homemade food that you can serve without second thoughts.

Storage & Shelf Life
  • Sweets: 7–10 days at room temperature (varies by item).
  • Pickles: 3–6 months in a dry, airtight container.
  • podis: 3–6 months in a dry, airtight container.
  • Snacks: 30–45 days in a cool, dry place.
